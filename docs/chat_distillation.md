# Chat Distillation – Fusion Blanket TCT Thread

## Key insight

High TBR alone is not a valid objective. Many top-performing configurations were simply front-loading neutron interaction and leaking energy outward.

## Observed regimes

### Reflector-dominant (Be sandwich)
- Very high TBR (~2.1)
- Very low attenuation (~0.015)
- Poor transport control

### Balanced regime
- TBR ~1.5–1.7
- Attenuation ~0.08–0.1
- Strong radial gradient
- Much more physically meaningful

## What actually matters
- radial flux distribution (not single-cell sampling)
- edge-based attenuation
- gradient through blanket
- energy retention vs leakage

## Structural takeaway

Good blankets appear to follow:

multiplier → breeder → shaping → moderation → capture

not pure reflection loops.

## Current direction

Test hybrid stacks that:
- multiply early
- diffuse in breeder
- apply controlled absorption mid/outer

PbLi is now being tested as a front multiplier layer.
